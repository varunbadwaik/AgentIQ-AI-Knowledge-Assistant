# Frequently Asked Questions - Customer Support

## Account & Login

### How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, follow these steps:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a reset link (arrives within 5 minutes)
5. Click the link and create a new password

Note: Password reset links expire after 24 hours.

### How do I change my email address?
Navigate to Settings > Account > Email. Enter your new email address and click "Update". You'll need to verify the new email before the change takes effect.

### I'm locked out of my account
If you've been locked out after multiple failed login attempts:
- Wait 30 minutes for the automatic unlock
- Or contact support with your account email for immediate assistance
- We may ask security questions to verify your identity

## Billing & Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (for annual plans)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay

### How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select a reason for cancellation
4. Confirm the cancellation

Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. No refunds for partial months.

### Can I get a refund?
We offer refunds within 14 days of purchase if:
- You haven't used the service extensively
- You're on your first subscription
- You contact support with your request

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

## Product Features

### How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Data > Export. You can download:
- All your data as a ZIP file
- Specific date ranges
- Individual projects

Export processing may take up to 1 hour for large accounts.

### What are the storage limits?
| Plan | Storage Limit |
|------|--------------|
| Free | 500 MB |
| Pro | 10 GB |
| Business | 100 GB |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

### How do I invite team members?
In the Team section, click "Invite Member". Enter their email address and select their role:
- Viewer: Can view but not edit
- Editor: Can view and edit
- Admin: Full access including billing

## Technical Support

### The app is running slowly
Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Disable browser extensions
3. Try a different browser (we recommend Chrome or Firefox)
4. Check your internet connection
5. If problems persist, contact support with your browser version

### I found a bug - how do I report it?
We appreciate bug reports! Please email bugs@company.com with:
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshots or screen recordings
- Your browser and operating system
- Any error messages you see

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We use:
- 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- SOC 2 Type II certified data centers
- Regular security audits
- Two-factor authentication available
